DOF:
Solid body:
  Constant coma, linear astigmatism, field tilt, focus
First 10 mirror modes add:
  Constant Astig, trefoil, quatrefoil, 2nd astig.
11 is special: linear coma and astig astig
12/13 adds constant pentafoil
14/15 adds linear 2nd astig, astigmatic trefoil
16/17 adds constant 2nd trefoil, linear quatrefoil,
18/19 adds constant hexfoil


So fit out in order:
  - Constant coma, linear astigmatism, field tilt, focus
  - Constant astig, trefoil, quatrefoil, 2nd astig
  - the rest

Note: Nonzero fiducial values:
  Constant focus,
  Linear coma,
  Astig astig
